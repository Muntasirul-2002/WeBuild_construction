We take care of all the challenges

At WeBuild, we understand that building a home is a big life decision and requires a significant investment of time and savings. However, the journey to build this dream home can be challenging:

We simplify complex processes

The journey of building a house involves many complex and technical procedures like getting various drawings made, obtaining approvals from regional authorities, buying numerous materials, etc. It is very difficult to get all these done on time and within budget.

We arrange all vendors

Building a house requires onboarding multiple vendors like architects, engineers, civil contractors, plumbers, electricians, painters, etc.Selecting the right vendors and coordinating with them to work in synchronization is a challenging task

We procure all materials

Building a home requires huge number of materials of correct quality, grade, and specification. Hence, material selection can be very complex and time consuming.

Complete security of payment

As you select a local contractor for construction of your home, once you make an advance payment to initiate the construction, there is no black and white reference document for the payment made. The transactions take place on the basis of goodwill and a level of mutual trust with the said contractor.
